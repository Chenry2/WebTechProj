Overview
The Acura NSX is a sports car developed and manufactured by the Honda. This car is the second generation of the NSX after being discontinued in 2005. The car is powered by an twin-turbo 3.5L V6 engine along with three electric motors. It features an all-wheel drive system and a 9-speed dual clutch transmission. Its aluminum alloy body make it fairly lightweight for its size, with only 3,803 lbs.
